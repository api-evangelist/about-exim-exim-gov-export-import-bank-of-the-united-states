<p>Last month, the Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM) held its 2025 Annual Conference: Winning Globally in America’s Golden Age. During the conference, twelve businesses were recognized as <a href="https://www.exim.gov/news/export-import-bank-united-states-recognizes-twelve-customers-exporters-year-2025-annual">Exporters of the Year</a>. These exporters represent innovation and the ability to compete globally. They also illustrate how EXIM’s trade finance tools and programs, such as <a href="https://www.exim.gov/solutions/export-credit-insurance">Export Credit Insurance</a> and <a href="https://www.exim.gov/solutions/working-capital">Working Capital Loan Guarantees</a> can assist American businesses in being successful on a global scale.</p>
