# Export-Import Bank of the United States (about-exim-exim-gov-export-import-bank-of-the-united-states)
The U.S. Export-Import Bank (EXIM) is the official export credit agency of the United States federal government. It assists in financing and facilitating U.S. exports of goods and services by providing export credit insurance, working capital guarantees, and direct loans to help American businesses compete in the global marketplace.
